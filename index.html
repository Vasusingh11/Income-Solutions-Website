About Income Solutions Inc

Income Solutions Inc is a Wyoming corporation engaged in the business of private lending. The Company focuses on originating, acquiring, and managing loans that are generally secured by real estate or other tangible business assets.

The Company seeks to deploy capital into income-producing lending opportunities and manages its portfolio through structured underwriting, disciplined risk assessment, and ongoing monitoring of its lending activities.

President: Cedric Kay Smiley-Peete

What We Do

Private Lending

Income Solutions Inc originates and holds loans primarily to small and medium-sized businesses and real estate operators. These loans are structured to meet specific commercial or project-based financing needs.

Asset-Focused Underwriting

Loan decisions are based primarily on collateral value, deal structure, and asset quality rather than solely on borrower creditworthiness.

Income Generation

The Company generates revenue primarily through interest earned on loans held on its balance sheet and, in certain cases, origination fees associated with lending transactions.

Overview of Debt Securities

From time to time, Income Solutions Inc may issue fixed-rate debt securities in the form of promissory notes. These notes represent unsecured obligations of the Company and are intended for investors who do not require immediate liquidity.

The notes are not publicly traded, are subject to restrictions on transferability, and are offered only pursuant to exemptions from registration under applicable federal and state securities laws.

Key Characteristics

  • Fixed-rate promissory notes
  • Sixty (60) month stated maturity from the issue date
  • Interest payable quarterly, either in cash or reinvested at the investor's election
  • Redemption available after an initial holding period, subject to advance written notice
  • Unsecured obligations of the Company
  • Senior to equity interests and junior to secured creditors

Use of Capital

Net proceeds from the issuance of debt securities are intended to be used for general corporate purposes, including but not limited to originating and acquiring secured and asset-based loans and funding working capital needs of the Company.

Investor Reporting

Income Solutions Inc provides periodic performance reporting that may include high-level lending metrics such as loan volume originated, default rates, and missed payment statistics.

A summarized balance sheet may be provided to investors periodically through electronic delivery or upon request.

Risk Factors

Investments in private debt securities involve a high degree of risk. The promissory notes issued by the Company are unsecured obligations, are not insured or guaranteed by any governmental agency, are not publicly traded, and may be illiquid for an extended period of time.

The Company's ability to make payments depends on its financial performance, borrower repayment behavior, market conditions, and other factors described in the applicable offering documents.

Prospective investors should carefully review all risk factors and consult their own legal, tax, and financial advisors before making any investment decision.

Investor Suitability

Any investment in the Company's debt securities is intended only for investors who meet applicable suitability standards, including financial sophistication and the ability to bear the economic risk of a long-term, illiquid investment.

Securities are offered only pursuant to applicable exemptions from registration and only to investors deemed suitable by the Company.
